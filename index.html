How these estimates work

This comparison illustrates how household electricity expenses can change over time when remaining on standard utility service versus selecting a solar power option. Calculations begin with the amounts you enter and apply an annual adjustment to reflect typical changes in rates over time.

  • Utility costs are projected using a conservative 9% annual adjustment based on long-term price trends observed in Maine.
  • Many solar agreements include a modest annual adjustment. For reference, fixed escalators near 2.99% or 3.59% per year are commonly available; choose the option that best matches your offer.
  • Use the timeline to review 1–30 years of expected cost exposure.

Figures shown are estimates for bill-to-bill comparison. Actual results may vary by usage, taxes, fees, and site conditions. Production and system output are not reflected here. Please review your documentation for complete terms.
